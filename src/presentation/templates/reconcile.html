Upload Bank Statement (CSV)

Most banks offer CSV export under Account Activity or Statements. The file should have columns for Date, Description, and Amount (or Debit/Credit). Checking/savings: export from the bank website. Credit cards: export from the card portal. Mortgage and retirement statements are stored for reference (no Plaid data to compare).
